# Schink Stories Email Communication Overview

Generated: 2026-06-12

## Summary

Schink Stories sends emails in four main situations:

- When someone contacts Schink Stories through the website.
- When someone buys something from the store.
- When someone starts or manages a subscription.
- When someone needs help with account access, such as password recovery or email confirmation.

The system also sends a small number of internal operational emails to help the team monitor important events, such as paid orders or subscription payment issues.

No customer personal data is included in this report.

## Customer Emails

These are the emails that customers or website visitors may receive.

| Email | Who receives it | When it is sent | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Contact form auto-reply | Website visitor | After they send a message through the contact form | Confirms that Schink Stories received their message |
| Store order confirmation | Store customer | After a store order payment is successful | Confirms the order and delivery details |
| Checkout reminder | Customer who started checkout | If they start checkout but do not complete payment | Reminds them that their checkout can still be completed |
| Subscription confirmation | Subscriber | After a paid subscription is confirmed | Confirms their subscription, plan, and billing details |
| Subscription payment recovery | Subscriber | If a recurring subscription payment fails | Helps the subscriber update payment details or continue the subscription |
| Subscription ended confirmation | Subscriber | When subscription access ends, expires, or is cancelled | Confirms the status change and explains access expectations |
| Password recovery | Account holder | When they request a password reset, or a team member sends one on their behalf | Gives them a secure link to reset their password |
| Email address confirmation | Account holder | When they request to change their account email address | Confirms the email address change securely |
| Signup confirmation, when required | New account holder | Only if account confirmation is required by the account system | Confirms their account email address |

## Internal Team Emails

These emails are sent to the Schink Stories team or support inbox, not to customers.

| Email | Who receives it | When it is sent | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Contact form notification | Schink Stories team | When a visitor sends a website contact message | Lets the team respond to the enquiry |
| Paid store order notification | Schink Stories team | When a store order payment is successful | Alerts the team that an order needs attention or fulfilment |
| Operational alert | Schink Stories team | When important subscription or payment events occur | Helps the team monitor payments, recoveries, and subscription changes |

## Customer Journey View

| Customer action or event | Email experience |
| --- | --- |
| A visitor sends a website enquiry | They receive a polite confirmation, and the team receives the enquiry |
| A customer pays for a store order | The customer receives an order confirmation, and the team receives an order alert |
| A customer starts checkout but does not finish | The system can send follow-up reminders with a link back to checkout |
| A customer subscribes successfully | The customer receives confirmation of their subscription |
| A subscription payment fails | The system can help the subscriber recover the payment before access ends |
| A subscription ends | The customer receives confirmation that the subscription status has changed |
| A customer forgets their password | The customer receives a secure password recovery email |
| A customer changes their account email address | The customer receives an email confirmation flow |

## Follow-Up Timing

Some emails are sent immediately, while others are scheduled as reminders.

| Email type | Timing |
| --- | --- |
| Contact form auto-reply | Immediately after the contact form is submitted |
| Store order confirmation | Immediately after successful payment confirmation |
| Subscription confirmation | After the subscription payment is confirmed |
| Password recovery | Immediately after the request is accepted |
| Email address confirmation | Immediately after the request is accepted |
| Checkout reminders | Scheduled follow-ups after checkout is started but not completed |
| Subscription payment recovery | Sent when payment recovery is needed, with follow-up reminders if the issue is not resolved |

## What This Means For Customers

The email flow is designed to:

- Confirm important customer actions.
- Help customers complete purchases and subscriptions.
- Support secure account access.
- Reduce missed payments and accidental loss of access.
- Keep the Schink Stories team informed when action is needed.

The overall experience is practical and service-focused: customers receive confirmations, reminders, and recovery links only when they are relevant to something they have done or something affecting their account.
